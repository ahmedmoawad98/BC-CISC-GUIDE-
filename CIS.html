Computer Information Systems

Whose should pursue an Information Systems Degree?

Recommended for students who want to use their computing skills in the field of business and organizational management. Students will be prepared for careers as Business Analyst, Systems Analyst, Database Administrator, Information Technology Specialist, and Information Consultant.

Helpful tips

  • Students must complete General Pathway Requirements along with the Computer Science major courses to complete their Bachelors of Science Degree in Computer Science.
  • To enroll in a CIS course, a student must have passed all prerequisite courses in the CIS department with a grade of C or higher.
  • To enroll in a Math course, a student must have passed all Math prerequisite courses with a D- or better.
  • Incoming students who have programming experience should consult a department advisor.
  • Any student who is not familiar with Microsoft Word, Excel, Access and/or PowerPoint is advised to also take CISC 1050 during his or her college studies.
