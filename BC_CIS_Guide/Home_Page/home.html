Meet the Team

Who We Are

We are a group of undergraduate CIS students who had a difficult time understanding our major requirements, choosing a career path, and finding resources to improve our tech skills. We created this interactive and user-friendly website to guide future CIS students with the information we learned throughout our time at Brooklyn College.

Mission

Our mission is to help CIS students understand their degree requirements while also providing a plethora of resources and tips.
